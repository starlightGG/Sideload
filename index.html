How to install

Setup guide — takes about 2 minutes

1
Tap Download AltForge above and allow the profile to install in Settings → General → VPN & Device Management.
2
Open AltForge, sign in with any Apple ID (a burner works fine — your credentials are never stored).
3
Tap + and choose an IPA file from Files, or browse the built-in source above and tap GET.
4
AltForge signs and installs the app automatically. Apps re-sign every 7 days in the background — no action needed.
